Lady GAGA: A Legend

Who Is Lady Gaga?

Her career began in New York City's Lower East Side, where she performed in clubs and honed her craft. In 2008, she released her debut album The Fame, which included the smash hits Just Dance and Poker Face, catapulting her to global fame. Over the years, her music has evolved, touching on various genres, from dance-pop and electronic music to jazz and country.

Lady Gaga is known for her artistic reinvention. From her iconic meat dress at the 2010 MTV Video Music Awards to her groundbreaking performances at major award shows, she continuously challenges societal norms and expectations. Her fans, known as "Little Monsters," are fiercely loyal, and she has often used her platform to speak out about issues such as LGBTQ+ rights, mental health awareness, and gender equality.
